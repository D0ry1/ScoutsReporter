## Description
Scouts Reporter is a desktop tool for Scout volunteers — Leaders, Lead Volunteers and Trustees — that turns official Scouts membership data into clear, actionable compliance reports.

Sign in with your own Scouts membership account and instantly see, across the part of the hierarchy you're responsible for:

• Criminal record check (DBS) status — who is valid, expiring, expired, or has none
• Learning compliance — First Response, Safety and Safeguarding, with expiry tracking
• Permits
• An at-a-glance dashboard showing compliance percentages per unit and team

Reports are role-aware: people are only flagged for the checks their current role actually requires (based on POR). Honorary roles such as Presidents and Vice Presidents, and Trustees, are no longer wrongly flagged — so your percentages reflect reality.

Everything runs locally on your own computer. Your data and your members' data never leave your device except to communicate with the official Scouts services. There is no tracking and no telemetry.

Built by a volunteer, for volunteers.
